## 1. Summary

DLA Troop Support (Medical) item titled "65--MATTRESS,LITTER"
(NAICS 339113 / PSC 65), due 2026-06-17.

## 2. Disqualifying factors

- **Primary reason:** Product category mismatch. A litter mattress is a
  stretcher / field-litter pad (medical equipment), not institutional
  bedding.
- **Contributing reasons:** NAICS 339113 (surgical/medical instruments
  & supplies) and PSC 65 (medical/dental/veterinary supplies) confirm a
  medical-device classification, not mattress manufacturing (337910).

## 3. What would change a future bid/no-bid call

- Nothing for the current vendor profile. Litter/stretcher pads are a
  distinct medical-equipment category outside the product line.

## 4. Lessons captured

- "MATTRESS,LITTER" under NAICS 339113 / PSC 65 is a false-positive
  pattern; the litter qualifier plus a non-337910 NAICS signals medical
  equipment.
- Consider adding `litter` to the scoring caution vocabulary if these
  recur.
