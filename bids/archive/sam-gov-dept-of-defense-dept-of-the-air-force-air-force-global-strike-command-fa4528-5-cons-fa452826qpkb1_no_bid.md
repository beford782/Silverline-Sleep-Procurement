## 1. Summary

Combined Synopsis/Solicitation for mattresses **and protective
encasements** at Minot AFB, ND (NAICS 337910 / PSC 7210), due
2026-06-17.

## 2. Disqualifying factors

- **Primary reason:** Scope requires mattress protectors / protective
  encasements, which the vendor profile marks `mattress_protectors: no`.
- **Contributing reasons:** North Dakota is far outside the vendor's
  five-state service geography (TX/OK/LA/MS/AR); and the ~2-day federal
  turnaround left no room to qualify a subcontractor for the encasements.

## 3. What would change a future bid/no-bid call

- Adding mattress protectors / encasements to the product line (or a
  reliable subcontractor for them).
- A delivery footprint or freight partner that reaches the upper Midwest
  at federal-acceptable cost.

## 4. Lessons captured

- "Mattresses **and** protective encasements" bundles a product line the
  vendor does not carry; flag bundled-accessory scope early.
- Out-of-geography federal items with short fuses are reliable no-bids
  even at perfect NAICS match.
