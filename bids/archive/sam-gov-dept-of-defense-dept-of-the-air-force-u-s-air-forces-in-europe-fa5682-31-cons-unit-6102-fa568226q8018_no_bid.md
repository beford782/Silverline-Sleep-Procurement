## 1. Summary

Supply, delivery, **installation, and removal of 102 sets of dormitory
furniture** for the same USAFE buyer (31st Contracting Squadron, Aviano AB,
Italy) as the previously reviewed sibling solicitation **FA568226QA053** —
see [`its no-bid memo`](sam-gov-air-force-31cons-dormitory-furniture-fireproof-mattress-fa568226qa053_no_bid.md)
for the full structural analysis. This is the same buy shape; the sibling
review applies verbatim.

## 2. Disqualifying factors (per the QA053 analysis)

- **OCONUS geography:** delivery to Aviano AB, Italy — outside the
  TX/OK/LA/MS/AR/NM own-fleet dock-delivery service area; no international
  freight/customs/in-theater logistics capability.
- **Scope — not a mattress buy:** complete furniture *sets* plus on-site
  installation and removal; Continental does not offer install/removal and
  does not manufacture the case goods that make up a set.
- **Timing:** SAM registration Submitted (2026-07-07) but not yet Active —
  no federal offer submittable regardless of fit.

## 3. What would change a future call

Nothing for this buyer class: per the QA053 lesson, OCONUS contracting
squadrons (USAFE/PACAF) are a **structural** geography no-fit at triage.
The genuine fit remains CONUS DoD dormitory/barracks mattress buys
(NAICS 337910 / PSC 7210, dock delivery, no install) once SAM is Active.
