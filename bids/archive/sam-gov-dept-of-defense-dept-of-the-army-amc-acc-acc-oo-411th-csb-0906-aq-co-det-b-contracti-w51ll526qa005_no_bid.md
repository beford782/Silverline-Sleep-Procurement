## 1. Summary

A direct federal **mattress and box spring purchase** — the cleanest product
fit the SAM funnel has surfaced (fit 100 when scored; flagged 2026-06-30 as
the highest-priority SAM-blocked row and a candidate for Silverline's first
real federal bid).

## 2. Disqualifying factor — timing only

**SAM registration not Active by the due date.** The entity registration was
submitted 2026-07-07 under the corrected LLC name; the DLA CAGE review letter
arrived 2026-07-09 and was answered the same day, but CAGE assignment and
Active status cannot realistically land before offers close 2026-07-10.
Without an Active SAM registration (FAR 52.204-7), a compliant offer cannot
be accepted. Product fit, geography, and delivery were NOT disqualifying.

This is the second pure SAM-timing loss (after USCG Base Boston
37010PR260000078, June) — and should be the last: activation is expected
within days.

## 3. What would change a future bid/no-bid call

Nothing needs to change except time: once SAM is **Active**, this exact
buy shape (direct DoD mattress/box-spring purchase, NAICS 337910 / PSC 7210,
CONUS) is the go signal — bid the next one. The 411th CSB is also a repeat
mattress buyer; watch for their re-solicitations and any re-post of this
requirement if it goes unawarded.

## 4. Lessons captured

- The funnel + registration race came down to ~1 week. The blocker chain
  (docs → EVS → wrong entity name → correction → CAGE) consumed the window;
  every step was addressed same-day once identified. Keep registrations
  healthy so bids never wait on plumbing again.
