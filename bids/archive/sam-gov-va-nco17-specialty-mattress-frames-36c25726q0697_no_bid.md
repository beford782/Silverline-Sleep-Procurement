## 1. Summary

SAM.gov Combined Synopsis/Solicitation for specialty mattresses and
frames at Amarillo VAHCS. The opportunity is current and includes 14
specialty mattresses plus 2 bariatric frames, with response due
2026-07-08, but the attachment review showed a clinical powered
medical-equipment requirement rather than a standard institutional
mattress supply opportunity.

## 2. Disqualifying factors

- The requirement is clinical therapeutic equipment under NAICS 339113 /
  PSC 6530, not core mattress manufacturing / institutional bedding.
- The requested product set is named / specialized equipment: BARIMATT
  1000 PLUS, Cair 1000, DERMAFLOAT APL MATTRESS SYSTEM, and an RC750
  rail/frame item.
- The solicitation requires an OEM authorized-distributor letter.
- Performance scope includes factory-trained or certified technicians,
  installation/removal/training, maintenance, inspections, monthly logs,
  and a five-year warranty.
- Full SAM.gov All Awards registration is still not active, so the entity
  is not federal-award ready even if the product fit were stronger.

## 3. What would change a future bid/no-bid call

Reconsider only if Continental Silverline has a qualified OEM medical
equipment distribution/service partner that can cover the named
low-air-loss and bariatric systems, certified technicians, warranty, and
ongoing service obligations. Otherwise, route VA clinical low-air-loss,
powered, bariatric-frame, and hospital-bed equipment notices to no-bid.

## 4. Lessons captured

- VA "mattress" notices can be true mattress demand, but this one is a
  medical-equipment/service package, not a normal institutional mattress
  or dorm/correctional bedding buy.
- Continue watching VA/VHA for standard institutional and mental-health
  mattress buys, while filtering out powered hospital-bed and
  named-system clinical equipment unless a partner is already available.
