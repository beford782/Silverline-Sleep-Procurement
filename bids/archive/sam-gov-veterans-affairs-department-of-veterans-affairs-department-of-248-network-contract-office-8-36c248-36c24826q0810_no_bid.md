mattresses at the Tampa VA Mental Health Clinic, FL (NAICS 337910 /
PSC 7195), due 2026-06-19.

## 2. Disqualifying factors

- **Primary reason:** Product category mismatch. The vendor profile
  marks `behavioral_health_mattress: no`. Behavioral-health mattresses
  carry specialized anti-ligature / suicide-resistant construction the
  vendor does not currently manufacture.
- **Contributing reasons:** Florida is outside the five-state service
  geography (TX/OK/LA/MS/AR).

## 3. What would change a future bid/no-bid call

- Adding a qualified behavioral-health / anti-ligature mattress line.
- A Southeast delivery footprint or freight partner.

## 4. Lessons captured

- "Mental health mattress" implies anti-ligature / behavioral-health
  construction — a distinct, regulated product, not a relabeled
  institutional mattress.
- Keep behavioral-health scope a no-bid until the product line and
  certifications exist.
