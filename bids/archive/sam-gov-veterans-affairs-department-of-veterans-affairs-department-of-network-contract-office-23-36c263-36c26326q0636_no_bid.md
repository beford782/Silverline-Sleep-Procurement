## 1. Summary

SAM.gov row for hospital grade mattress inspection services in Saint
Cloud, Minnesota. The scope is service-only inspection work, not a
request to manufacture, supply, or deliver mattresses.

## 2. Disqualifying factors

- **Primary reason:** Service-only inspection contract, outside the
  vendor's product supply lane.
- **Contributing reasons:** Out-of-geography location and NAICS 561210
  services classification.

## 3. What would change a future bid/no-bid call

- A separate inspection-services business line or a teaming partner for
  VA facility-management service work.

## 4. Lessons captured

- Keep `inspection services` in the scoring caution vocabulary.
- Titles combining "mattress" with inspection/service language should
  be triaged before drafting.
