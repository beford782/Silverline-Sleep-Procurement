# Portal Checklists

Per-vendor plans for registering on opportunity portals and picking the
right commodity codes.

## Why a per-vendor checklist

Most of the work in standing up a new vendor is portal hygiene:
registering on CMBL, Bonfire, IonWave, Beacon Bid, the cooperatives,
and SAM.gov; selecting the right NIGP / commodity codes on each; and
building saved searches that surface relevant solicitations.

A per-vendor checklist captures:

1. The portals to register on, in priority order
2. The commodity-term groups to feed into each portal's category picker
3. The saved searches to run weekly
4. A registration field tracker (URL, vendor ID, notification email,
   documents uploaded, renewal date)

## Reusable sections

The "Commodity-Code Cleanup Strategy" and "Suggested Saved Searches"
sections in `continental_silverline_portal_setup.md` are vendor-agnostic
for institutional mattress vendors. Copy them verbatim when seeding a
new vendor's checklist, then narrow them down based on the vendor's
product fit.

## File naming

`<vendor>_portal_setup.md`, lowercased, underscore-separated.
