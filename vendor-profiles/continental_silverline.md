## 8. Immediate Setup Gaps to Close

1. Add company website or catalog link.
2. Confirm exact warranty terms by product category.
3. Confirm minimum order quantities by product category.
4. List exact fire-safety standards and testing documents for each institutional product line.
5. Confirm whether any HUB, MBE, WBE, DBE, small business, local business, ISO, or other certifications apply.
6. Decide minimum attractive contract size.
7. Decide ideal contract size.
8. Define no-bid conditions, including delivery requirements, insurance limits, sample requirements, payment terms, and price-hold requirements.
9. Consider whether inside delivery, room-by-room delivery, old mattress removal, or installation can be added through internal service or subcontractors.
10. Build price-escalation language for foam and other raw materials before pursuing multi-year fixed-price contracts.

## 9. Recommended First Workstream

### Week 1: Portal completion and commodity-code cleanup

- Verify Texas CMBL commodity/NIGP codes.
- Complete Harris County Bonfire registration.
- Complete Houston ISD IonWave registration.
- Confirm City of Houston Beacon Bid notifications.
- Build university portal target list for UT System, Texas A&M System, University of Houston, Texas Southern University, community colleges, and regional public universities.

### Week 2: Bid asset package

- Build one-page capability statement.
- Assemble product specification sheets.
- Assemble fire-safety and compliance documents.
- Draft a standard public-sector delivery statement.
- Draft a standard warranty statement.
- Create City of Austin past-performance summary.

### Week 3: Opportunity research

- Search ESBD / Texas SmartBuy for mattress, bedding, dormitory, correctional, shelter, medical, and furniture terms.
- Search City of Houston, Harris County, HISD, local counties, nearby cities, universities, and cooperatives.
- Pull awarded/expired contracts for mattresses, bed frames, dorm furnishings, jail mattresses, and shelter bedding.

### Week 4: Pursuit pipeline

- Score each opportunity as bid / no-bid / monitor.
- Draft Q&A questions for restrictive specs.
- Prepare first response package using reusable templates.
- Track due dates, addenda, required forms, and submission portals.
